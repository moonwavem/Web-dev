1. Bora Bora, French Polynesia: A Tropical Paradise

“The stunning landscapes, exceptional snorkeling opportunities, and warm, welcoming people all contribute to an unforgettable experience. The highlight of my visit was swimming with humpback whales on two consecutive days, an extraordinary memory that will stay with me forever.”
The best time to visit: May, or June because there is much less chance of rain, and the weather is a comfortable 28-29 degrees Celsius.

French Polynesia is considered one of the safest places in the Oceana and also our number one most exotic and beautiful destination on our exotic list. It’s a popular destination for luxury travelers. Bora Bora in French Polynesia is a tropical paradise with its crystal-clear waters and iconic overwater bungalows. This exotic location offers an exclusive experience surrounded by stunning natural beauty. The island is famous for its vibrant marine life and pristine beaches, including the famous Matira Beach, making it a perfect place for water sports enthusiasts and those seeking a serene escape amidst palm bungalows and turquoise lagoons on the Tahiti peninsula.
